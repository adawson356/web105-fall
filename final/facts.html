Sun Facts

The earth is puny

One million Earths could fit inside the sun. The sun is a yellow dwarf star. As suns go our sun is of an average size. A sun called Betelgeuse is a red giant sun that is 700 times bigger and 14 thousand times brighter than our sun.

The sun is a middle aged star

The sun and the planets formed around 4.6 billion years ago. In about 5 billion years it will enter the red giant phase. Its always harder to be trim after the middle years. In this phase it will swell up and consume the earth. Later it will shrink down to be a tiny white dwarf.

The sun is heating up

The sun is becoming increasingly luminous. In the next billion years the sun will become ten percent more luminous. The hotter and brighter sun will eventually cause the earth surface to be free of water.

Atmosphere of the Sun

The sun chromosphere is around sixteen times more heated than the the sun's surface. The corona beyond the sun is sixteen thousand times more heated than the the sun's surface. The light seen around the moon during the eclipse is the super hot corona.

Yellow and Blue

The sun we see is yellow because we see the longer wavelengths of light that make it hrough the earth's atmosphere. The earth's sky appears blue since blue and violet short wavelengths are absorbed by our atmosphere.

The Sun is Glue

The sun holds the solar system together. It's gravitational pull is 28 times more powerful than earths. The sun contains 99.86% of the mass of the solar system.
