Energy

Water from the water treatment plant is pumped into the water tower. The higher the water tower is that is filled with water, the higher the potential energy of that water. Even with no electricity, gravity will supply water and water pressure. Fire fighters and households have water available despite lack of power. That is until the tank runs out of water!

PE Potential Energy and Water Pressure

Potential Energy

Potential energy is the energy possessed by a body by virtue of its position relative to others, stresses within itself, electric charge, and other factors

Water Pressure

Water pressure translates into force per unit area exerted by the weight of water. Water towers supply water pressure. Everyone can appreciate water pressure when they don't have it!
