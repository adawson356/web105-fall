Working Together

Water towers save money by managing peak water demand. When the majority of people are taking showers in the morning, the water tower supplies additional water. At night when water usage is low, water from the water treatment plant is pumped into the water tower.

Water Treatment Plant and Water Tower

Water Treatment

The municipal water treatment plant removes sediment (see picture below) and otherwise treats the water so that is potable. Then this clean water is sent through a high-lift pump. The water tower is connected to the pump via the system’s primary feeder pipes.
